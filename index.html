Duckenfield Elementary School

The Duckenfield Elementary School is an institution in the vicinity of Killeen, Texas providing education for students from the ages of 6 through to 11. The Duckenfield Elementary School is very pleased to be an institution that is notable for our capacity to quicken the academic performance of our understudies while giving a supporting climate to learning. The school has been awarded for the last 10 years the "Honors Title". This award is given to schools that have demonstrated success in significantly closing the achievement gap between high and low-performing students. Our profoundly prepared teachers at the institution are gifted at defining thorough scholastic objectives for students and are completely dedicated to working with them to arrive at those objectives.
